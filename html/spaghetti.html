Indian-style Spaghetti

    What you will need?

  • 2 tbsp light cooking oil
  • 1 large onion finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp red-chilli paste
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves
  • 2 large tomatoes finely chopped
  • salt to taste
  • 2 capcicum chooped(different colour)
  • 500 gms whole-grain spaghetti cooked, drained and set aside

    Directions :

  1. Heat the oil in a broad non-stick, add onions and ginger-garlic paste & saute on medium flame for about 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add tomatoes, capcisum and 2tb spoon water & saute for about a minute.
  3. Add soy sauce, chilli sauce, turmeric and salt to taste.
  4. Add cooked spaghetti, mix gently and cook on medium flame for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Garnish the spaghetti with chopped coriander leaves.
