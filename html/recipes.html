Multigrain-puri

Beautiful golden brown Puris made with multigrain flour, served with accompaniments of choice!

Kosambari

kosambari is a delicious, protein rich lentil and good for weight loss. It is a must side dish of every Indian festive meal.

Girmit

A popular spicy north karnataka street food recipe prepared from puffed rice or murmura. rice puff is known with several names, but popularly it is associated with bhelpuri, churumuri or even mandakki recipes in kannada.

Masala-vade

Masala vade is popular South Indian Tea time Snack. It is also known as chana dal vade in some parts of India. These are crispy and are enjoyed with a cup of Tea

Besan-Vade

Besan-vade/Jhunka Vade/Zunka Vade/Jhunka is a traditional dish from North Karnataka and Maharashtra prepared using gram flour, onion and spices. This tasty dish is used as an accompaniment with chapathi or Jowar Roti.
