Besan-vade/Jhunka

    What you will need?

  • 1 cup gram flour
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp Cumin seeds
  • 1 Onion chopped
  • 1 Tomato chopped
  • 1 tbsp Mustard seeds
  • 1/4 tbsp turmeric
  • 2 tbsp chilli powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • coriander leaves
  • Grated coconut
  • 2 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 2 tbsp Jaggery

    Directions :

  1. Mix water and gram flour to make a smooth paste and keep aside.
  2. Heat the oil, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds and curry leaves.
  3. Add onions & saute on medium flame for about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes and saute for a minute.
  5. Add turmeric powder, chilli powder and salt.
  6. Add tamarind paste and jaggery.
  7. Add gram flour paste and cook for 5 minutes.
  8. Turn off the heat once the mixture is thickened.
  9. Transfer the mixture to greased pan/tray and spread the mixture evenly.
  10. Sprinkle some fresh coconut and coriander leaves. Let it cool completely.
  11. Cut the mixture into square or diamond shapes.
