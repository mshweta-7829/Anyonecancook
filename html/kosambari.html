Kosambari/Salad

    What you will need?

  • 1 grated carrot (small)
  • 250gms of soaked Chana dal(Chickpeas)
  • 100gms of grated coconut
  • 2 tbsp red-chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves
  • salt to taste

    Directions :

  1. Take Chana dal in a bowl.
  2. Add all the above mentioned ingredients.
  3. This can be served with Chapatis or Holige/Obbattu/Puran-Poli
