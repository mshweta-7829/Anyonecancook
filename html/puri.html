Multigrain-Puri

    What you will need?

  • 250gms of wheat and jowar flour
  • 100gms of Besan, rice and corn flour
  • 1 tbsp Cumin seeds
  • 2 tbsp red-chilli powder
  • 1/4 tbsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves
  • cooking oil to fry
  • salt to taste

    Directions :

  1. Add all the flours.
  2. Add turmeric powder, chilli powder, cumin seeds and coriander leaves.
  3. Knead the dough.
  4. Make small balls and roll out into small puris.
  5. fry the puris in hot oil.
